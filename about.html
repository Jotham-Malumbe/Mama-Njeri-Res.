About Mama Njeri Restaurant

Where tradition meets great taste in Kitale Market

Our Story

Mama Njeri Restaurant was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to serve fresh, affordable, and delicious home-cooked meals to the people of Kitale.

What started as a small food kiosk at Kitale Market has grown into a beloved local restaurant known for its generous portions, authentic flavors, and warm hospitality.

What Makes Us Special

Fresh Ingredients

We use only the freshest ingredients sourced daily from Kitale Market.

Fast Service

Your order is prepared fresh when you order – never pre-cooked.

Family Feel

Every customer is treated like family. That’s our promise.

Authentic Taste

Recipes passed down through generations with Mama Njeri’s special touch.
